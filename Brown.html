A.J. Brown - 2023 Season Highlights

Philadelphia Eagles

A.J. Brown had an incredible season in 2023, solidifying his place as one of the top wide receivers in the NFL. Known for his explosive speed and strong hands, Brown was a key player for the Philadelphia Eagles, helping the team achieve significant success throughout the season.

In 2023, Brown amassed over 1,400 receiving yards and scored 11 touchdowns, showcasing his ability to make big plays when it mattered most. His chemistry with quarterback Jalen Hurts was evident, as they connected on numerous deep passes that electrified fans at Lincoln Financial Field.

The Philadelphia Eagles, with their iconic midnight green and silver, are proud to have A.J. Brown as a cornerstone of their offense as they continue to strive for championship glory.
